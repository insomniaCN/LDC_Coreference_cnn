<HEADLINE> 
Sevilla winger Navas out with leg injury 
< / HEADLINE> 
<DATELINE> 
SEVILLE , Spain 2010-09-18 13:11:20 UTC 
< / DATELINE> 
<TEXT> 
<P> 
Sevilla winger Jesus Navas will be out for up to two weeks because of a muscle injury .
< / P> 
<P> 
The Spain international sustained the injury to his left leg during Sevilla 's 1 - 0 Europa League defeat against French club PSG on Thursday .
< / P> 
<P> 
Navas joins Renato on the sidelines after the Brazilian midfielder also picked up an ankle injury during the match and will be out for more than two weeks .
< / P>

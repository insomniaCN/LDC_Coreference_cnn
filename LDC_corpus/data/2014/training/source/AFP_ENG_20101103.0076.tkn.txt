<HEADLINE> 
Santander names new Banesto chief after Botin move 
< / HEADLINE> 
<DATELINE> 
MADRID , Nov 3 , 2010 ( AFP ) - 
< / DATELINE> 
<TEXT> 
<P> 
Spanish banking giant Santander said Wednesday it has proposed board member Antonio Basagoiti as the non-executive chairman of its affiliate Banesto .
< / P> 
<P> 
The move follows the appointment earlier Wednesday of Banesto 's executive chairwoman Ana Patricia Botin as the new chief executive of Santander UK .
< / P> 
<P> 
Banco Santander ’s executive committee " agreed to propose to Banesto ’s board of directors the appointment of Antonio Basagoiti as the non-executive chairman of Banesto 's board and to its executive committee , " Santander said in a statement .
< / P> 
<P> 
Basagoiti is a former chairman of Spanish utility Union Fenosa .
< / P> 
<P> 
The decision was taken during a meeting of the executive committee to decide on Botin 's appointment .
< / P> 
<P> 
At Santander UK , she is replacing Antonio Horta - Osorio , who was poached by Britain 's state - rescued Lloyds bank to be its new current chief executive .
< / P>

<P> 
Turkish police seize large amount of heroin 
< / P> 
<P> 
ANKARA , March 4 ( Xinhua ) -- Turkish police seized a large amount 
of heroin in eastern and southeastern Turkey in the past two 
days , the semi-official Anatolia news agency reported on 
Thursday .
< / P> 
<P> 
The police seized 134.5 kg of heroin Thursday in an abandoned car 
in the eastern province of Van , according to the report .
< / P> 
<P> 
Residents of an apartment complex who got suspicious of a car 
parked in their garage informed the police , said the report .
< / P> 
<P> 
The police team searched the abandoned car with sniffer dogs and 
found 134.5 kg of heroin in concealed compartments .
< / P> 
<P> 
The police later arrested two suspects who came to the parking 
lot to pick up the car , said the report .
< / P> 
<P> 
Meanwhile , acting on a tip - off , the Turkish police stopped a 
truck at Hakkari - Van highway and found 117.9 kg of heroin in the 
vehicle in the southeastern province of Hakkari on Wednesday .
< / P> 
<P> 
Turkey is a key transit route for smuggling drugs from Asia and 
the Middle East to markets in western Europe .
< / P> 
<P> 
Turkish anti-narcotic teams seized 12.2 tons of heroin , 25.8 tons 
of hashish and 391,770 ecstasy pills in operations throughout the 
country in 2009 , according to Anatolia .
< / P>

<HEADLINE> 
CATHERINE E . F . BIDEN , 92 , MOTHER OF THE VICE PRESIDENT 
< / HEADLINE> 
<TEXT> 
<P> 
WASHINGTON -- Catherine Biden , the mother of Vice President Joe 
Biden and a presence on the campaign trail with him in 2008 , died 
Friday at the Biden home in Wilmington , Del . She was 92 .
< / P> 
<P> 
Her death was announced by the vice president . She had become 
seriously ill in the last few days , and her son had left Washington 
on Thursday to be with her .
< / P> 
<P> 
Joe Biden often paid tribute to his mother in speeches and in 
his 2007 autobiography , " Promises to Keep , " lauding her for her 
support in his political and personal life ( though he noted that 
she had expressed reservations when he first discussed going into 
politics ) . In his successful first race for the Senate in 1972 , 
when he was 30 , she organized coffee gatherings .
< / P> 
<P> 
When Joe Biden sought the Democratic presidential nomination in 
2008 , Catherine Biden campaigned with him in Iowa before the 
caucuses there . She also campaigned with him before the November 
election in Scranton , Pa . , and at the University of Delaware in 
Newark .
< / P> 
<P> 
Catherine Biden was in the audience when her son accepted the 
vice presidential nomination at the Democratic National Convention 
in Denver on Aug . 27 , 2008 .
< / P> 
<P> 
In his speech , Joe Biden said she had helped and encouraged him 
in overcoming his stuttering as a youth , telling him that he 
stuttered " because I was so bright , I could n't get the thoughts 
out quickly enough . " 
< / P> 
<P> 
After his wife and daughter were killed in an auto accident in 
December 1972 , Joe Biden said , " She told me , 'Joey , God sends no 
cross you can not bear . ' And when I triumphed , she was quick to 
remind me it was because of others . " 
< / P> 
<P> 
He added : " My mother 's creed is the American creed : No one is 
better than you . You are everyone 's equal , and everyone is equal to 
you . " 
< / P> 
<P> 
Catherine Eugenia Finnegan , who was known as Jean , was born on 
July 17 , 1917 , to Ambrose J . Finnegan and Geraldine C . Blewitt of 
Scranton , Pa . In 1941 she married Joseph Robinette Biden , who died 
in 2002 . Joe Biden , the first of their four children , was born in 
1942 .
< / P> 
<P> 
While the vice president has described his boyhood in postwar 
Scranton as idyllic , in fact his father suffered a number of 
business reversals , and for several years when Joe Jr . was young , 
the Bidens were forced to move in with his mother 's parents .
< / P> 
<P> 
Besides the vice president , Catherine Biden is survived by her 
daughter , Valerie Biden Owens of Kennett Square , Pa . ; her two 
younger sons , James , of Merion Station , Pa . , and Francis , of 
Florida ; and many grandchildren and great - grandchildren .
< / P>
